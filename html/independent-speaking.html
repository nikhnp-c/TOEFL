TOEFL Speaking

Do you think people should read the news to keep up with what is happening around the world, even if it may have nothing to do with their personal lives? Use details and examples to explain your opinion.

Some people believe old people should not take risks or participate in adventurous/challenging events as young people do. Do you agree or disagree, and why? Use details and examples to explain your opinion.

Some think robots can replace people for many types of work in the future, while others believe that robots will never be smarter or more valuable than humans. Which do you prefer? Use details and examples to explain your opinion.

Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? Some schools are considering eliminating computer labs on campus because all students nowadays have their laptops. Use details and examples to explain your opinion.

Some people like to read reviews before going to see a movie, in which critics offer their opinions of the movie. Other people prefer to watch films without reading reviews. Which do you prefer? Use details and examples to explain your opinion.
